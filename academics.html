Academic Details

Explore the academic programs and curriculum at Chokrojan School.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of academic programs designed to cater to the diverse needs of our students and provide a solid foundation for their future.

Primary Education

Our primary education program focuses on holistic development through engaging and interactive learning experiences.

Secondary Education

Our secondary education program prepares students for higher education and future careers through a rigorous curriculum.

Science Stream

Our science stream offers advanced courses in physics, chemistry, and biology, preparing students for careers in STEM fields.

Commerce Stream

Our commerce stream provides in-depth knowledge of business, economics, and accounting, fostering future business leaders.

Arts Stream

Our arts stream nurtures creativity and critical thinking through courses in humanities, literature, and fine arts.

ICT Stream

Our ICT stream equips students with practical skills in information technology and computer science, crucial for the digital age.

Curriculum Overview
